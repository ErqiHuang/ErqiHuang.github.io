About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in School of Electrical Science and Engineering, Nanjing University. I am studying in group of Computational Spectral Video Camera in CITE Lab, supervised by Prof. Xun Cao.

My research interests focus on computational photography and machine learning, especially for spectral video acquisition and reconstruction.
